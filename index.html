Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is TONI MARKET?

TONI MARKET is a decentralized P2P platform on the TON blockchain, allowing users to vote on token listings, trade securely, and participate in a deflationary token economy.

How does the burning mechanism work?

For every transaction in TONI tokens, a portion of the commission is burned, reducing the total supply and increasing the value of remaining tokens.

Can I trade other assets on TONI MARKET?

Yes, you can trade BTC, TON, and other assets directly with other users without the need for verification.
